JUPITER

Jupiter is the largest planet, showing many specific featureL enormous size and striking cloud bands in color. It is a gas giant, composed mainly of hydrogen and helium. Jupiter has a strong magnetic field and 79 moons. The four largest moons are called the Galilean moons, named after Galileo Galilei, who discovered them in 1610. Jupiter is the fourth brightest object in the solar system, after the Sun, the Moon, and Venus.
